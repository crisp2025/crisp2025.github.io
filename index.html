About CRISP 2025

The National Conference on Critical Metals: Recycling, Innovation, Separation, and Processing (CRISP 2025) brings together leading researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers to discuss the latest developments in critical metals technology.

This conference aims to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange in the field of critical metals, focusing on sustainable practices, innovative technologies, and future opportunities.

Department of Fuel, Minerals and Metallurgical Engineering

The Department of Fuel, Minerals and Metallurgical Engineering (FMME) at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad is one of the premier departments in India, renowned for its excellence in education, research, and innovation in the field of minerals and metallurgical engineering. Established with a rich legacy spanning several decades, the department has been at the forefront of advancing knowledge and technology in critical metals processing, mineral beneficiation, and sustainable metallurgical practices.

The department offers comprehensive programs from undergraduate to doctoral levels, focusing on cutting-edge research in areas such as extractive metallurgy, materials science, mineral processing, and environmental sustainability. With state-of-the-art laboratories and world-class faculty, FMME continues to contribute significantly to the advancement of metallurgical sciences and the development of sustainable technologies for the minerals industry.

IIT (ISM) Dhanbad

Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines) Dhanbad, commonly known as IIT (ISM) Dhanbad, is a prestigious public technical university located in Dhanbad, Jharkhand, India. Originally established in 1926 as the Indian School of Mines, it was granted the status of an Institute of National Importance in 2016 and became part of the IIT system.

IIT (ISM) Dhanbad has a distinguished history of over 95 years in providing quality education and conducting pioneering research in engineering, technology, and applied sciences. The institute is particularly renowned for its expertise in mining, petroleum, and earth sciences, while also excelling in various other engineering disciplines. With its sprawling campus, modern facilities, and strong industry connections, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad continues to be a leading center of excellence for technical education and research in India.

The institute has produced numerous distinguished alumni who have made significant contributions to academia, industry, and public service, both in India and internationally. Its commitment to innovation, research excellence, and societal impact makes it an ideal venue for hosting CRISP 2025.
